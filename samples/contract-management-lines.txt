MANAGEMENT LIABILITY REQUIREMENTS

The insured shall maintain Directors and Officers Liability insurance with a
limit of not less than $1,000,000 each claim and aggregate.

The insured shall maintain Employment Practices Liability insurance with a
limit of not less than $1,000,000.

The insured shall maintain Fiduciary Liability insurance with a limit of not
less than $1,000,000 for employee benefit plan claims.

The insured shall maintain Commercial Crime / Fidelity coverage, including
employee dishonesty, with a limit of not less than $500,000.

The insured shall maintain Cyber Liability and Technology Errors & Omissions
coverage with a limit of not less than $1,000,000. Coverage shall include
privacy liability, network security, data breach response, PCI/payment card
liability, media liability, cyber extortion/ransomware, dependent business
interruption, computer fraud, social engineering, and regulatory defense.
